Our Menu

This is a short list of our most popular beverages and pastries. We have a wider catalogue and prices in our cafe. Come for a visit!

Espresso

Strong black coffee made by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans. Works for breakfast when you are in a hurry and need a quick coffee.

Cappuccino

Coffee mixed with steamed milk and topped with foamed milk. Perfect for lunch or if you want sit for a cup of coffee.

Latte

Coffee with steamed milk and a small amount of frothed milk. Perfect for those who doesn't like their coffees that strong.

Ice Tea

Stay refreshed with our flavorful ice tea. We have lemon and peach options, it's a perfect drink if you want to relax or don't enjoy coffee much.

Chocolate Croissant

Sweet combination of flaky croissant dough and quality chocolate filling. This croissant is perfect for breakfast and chocolate lovers!

Hazelnut Cake

Layers of moist cake with hazelnut goodness and topped with a tasty hazelnut frosting. Go to option for everyone who likes nuts.

Butter Croissant

Delicately flaky layers of pastry with a buttery flavor. Timeless classic for any occasion.

Strawberry Cake

Moist layers of vanilla sponge cake are filled with fresh strawberry jam and topped with a sweet strawberry frosting. This cake slice is perfect for strawberry lovers!
